Welcome to 'Rapid JOBBS'

Rapid JOBBS was started in 2016 to provide technical and management consultancy services to the private and public sector in Sri Lanka by Geo-Informatics and Environmental science graduates. Our initial idea is to provide cost effective and efficient solution to the clients by using available satellite and spatial data in the field of environmental and natural resources management, business sector and other public sectors. We have enthusiastic team for collecting real time data and assist the research which deal with GIS and Remote sensing applications. We have very young and energetic MSc and BSc graduates and working on become the best service providers in this field in Sri Lanka.
